B-17G Flying Fortress

Significant Facts

1. This aircraft was the last B-17 assigned to the 381st Bomb Group at RAF Ridgewell, UK. It arrived in May 1945 but did not see combat.

2. Used in secret "Flying Bomb" tests in 1948.

3. One of the last B-17's in USAF service.

4. Last B-17 to drop bombs - used during airpower demonstrations.

5. This B-17 was on display at the Air Force Museum Dayton, OH from 1957 - 1989.

6. The aircraft is painted as "Sleep Time Gal" 42-107112, another B-17 assigned to the 381st BG.

7. Nose-art was painted in 1995 by noted aviation artist Gil Cohen.
